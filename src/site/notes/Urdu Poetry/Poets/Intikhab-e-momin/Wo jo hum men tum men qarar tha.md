वो जो हम में तुम में क़रार था तुम्हें याद हो कि न याद हो
वही या'नी वा'दा निबाह का तुम्हें याद हो कि न याद हो
	*The bond that we made as i recall, whether you remember or whether do you not
	That is to say the commitment to never fall, whether you remember or whether do you not*

---
	It is quite clear that the beloved has already betrayed the poet in some sense and he is stuck on the memories that they share and trying to comprehend how has the beloved changed, while doing so it also seems that he is thinking about since the promises she made were all farce(as stated about staying together), whether anything she said meant anything or holds any truth. 
	The whole ghazal is structured in a very rhetorical manner, i.e. he is talking to the beloved but does not expect her to answer. As if he is trying to think back to the time they had together to ease his pain or perhaps intensify it by juxtaposing the dreadful present to peaceful past. 

वो जो लुत्फ़ मुझ पे थे बेशतर वो करम कि था मिरे हाल पर
मुझे सब है याद ज़रा ज़रा तुम्हें याद हो कि न याद हो
	*The kindness often showered upon me, that grace shown to my plight*
	*I remember everything but fragments of it, whether you remember or whether do you not.* 
	*I remember everything to smallest detail, whether you remember or whether do you not.* 
	

---
	It is a trademark imagery of the classical Urdu ghazal that the poet's(lover's) life is hanging by a single strand i.e. the beloved's grace/mercy/pity. 
	The first line takes the reader back the memory lane of the poet. A time when the poet was in a very dire condition but beloved's grace was upon him so he was happy nonetheless. 
	The second line is a complaint but the complaint is worded in the most kindest of the words, a complaint about why the grace which was bestowed upon him is now being withheld. In a similar way, that of a child would complain if the mother has stopped patting his back. 
	
वो नए गिले वो शिकायतें वो मज़े मज़े की हिकायतें
वो हर एक बात पे रूठना तुम्हें याद हो कि न याद हो
	 Those
	 

---
	The poet is thinking back to the times when the beloved used to complain to him about every small thing and her sulking on everything he said, implying the level of affection she had for him as "you would only get mad on the people whom you know would try to win you back over", use pata tha ki wo jitni bar usse ruthegi, wo har bar use manayega hi. 

कभी बैठे सब में जो रू-ब-रू तो इशारतों ही से गुफ़्तुगू
वो बयान शौक़ का बरमला तुम्हें याद हो कि न याद हो
	

हुए इत्तिफ़ाक़ से गर बहम तो वफ़ा जताने को दम-ब-दम
गिला-ए-मलामत-ए-अक़रिबा तुम्हें याद हो कि न याद हो

कोई बात ऐसी अगर हुई कि तुम्हारे जी को बुरी लगी
तो बयाँ से पहले ही भूलना तुम्हें याद हो कि न याद हो

कभी हम में तुम में भी चाह थी कभी हम से तुम से भी राह थी
कभी हम भी तुम भी थे आश्ना तुम्हें याद हो कि न याद हो

सुनो ज़िक्र है कई साल का कि किया इक आप ने वा'दा था
सो निबाहने का तो ज़िक्र क्या तुम्हें याद हो कि न याद हो

कहा मैं ने बात वो कोठे की मिरे दिल से साफ़ उतर गई
तो कहा कि जाने मिरी बला तुम्हें याद हो कि न याद हो

वो बिगड़ना वस्ल की रात का वो न मानना किसी बात का
वो नहीं नहीं की हर आन अदा तुम्हें याद हो कि न याद हो
